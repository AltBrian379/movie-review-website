Would You Watch It Again?

Welcome to the only movie review website where we ask Would You Watch It Again?

We believe that the mainstream form of movie reviews don't capture what movie-goers want to know about a film! Instead, we determine a movie's quality on the grounds of a simple question.

Some movies make you feel like Once is Enough, some you'll only watch If Its The Only Thing On, while with others you'll be wanting to say "I Want My Money Back".

However, when a movie makes you think, "I Would Watch It Again", you know you got a good one on your hands. if it makes you want to watch it Religiously, you know you got something special!
